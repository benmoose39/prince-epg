3:15pm - 	Rage at Dawn
Based on the true-life exploits of the infamous Reno Brothers gang. The film opens with a planned bank raid going wrong; in revenge, the gang find and kill the private detective who set the ambush. Undeterred, the agency send their finest man James Barlow to Indiana where, posing as a train robber, he infiltrates the gang, but can he outwit the Reno Brothers as well as keeping his identity secret from the townsfolk?
