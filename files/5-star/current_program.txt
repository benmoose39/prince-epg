11:40pm - 	Austin Powers: The Spy Who Shagged Me
Austin Danger Powers returns with a new sidekick in Felicity Shagwell. Dr Evil has invented a time machine and gone back in time to steal Austin's sexual powers, leaving our hero in 1999 without the ability to perform. Not only does he have to rescue his libido, but he must also thwart Dr Evil's plan to devastate Earth with a moon-based laser.
