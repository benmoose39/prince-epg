10:15pm - 100 Days to Victory
The Allies win a great victory at Amiens. The tide is turning, but the victors are under no illusions. The German Army remains a daunting fighting force and it has an ace up the sleeve: the Hindenburg Line, the most formidable defensive system in military history. If the Allies are to have any hope for victory, they'll need to find a way to punch through Germany's wall of steel.
