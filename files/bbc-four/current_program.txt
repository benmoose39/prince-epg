7:00pm - Woolly Mammoth: Secrets from the Ice
Professor Alice Roberts reveals the natural history of the Ice Age's woolly mammoth. Mammoths have transfixed humans since the depths of the last Ice Age when their herds roamed across what is now Europe and Asia. Although extinct for thousands of years, scientists can now paint an incredibly detailed picture of their lives thanks to whole carcasses beautifully preserved in the Siberian permafrost.
