12:00pm - Bargain Hunt
Eric Knowles is in the Lancashire town of Eccleston, with experts Caroline Hawley and Roo Irvine guiding the red and blue teams through their shop, hoping to land the biggest profit and scoop the win at the auction. From laughing policemen to haunted houses, Eric re-discovers his youth at a vintage penny arcade, home to around 50 vintage arcade machines, fully restored by owner and collector Darren Hesketh.
