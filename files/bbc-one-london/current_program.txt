2:00pm - 
Gabby Logan hosts live coverage as Scotland continue their Six Nations campaign against defending champions Ireland in front of a partisan crowd at Murrayfield. Ireland have won their last ten Tests against the Scots and were victorious on their most recent Six Nations visit to Edinburgh in 2023, triumphing 22-7 on their way to winning a fourth Grand Slam and a 15th title. However, Scotland's last three wins over the Irish have all come at Murrayfield in this very championship.
