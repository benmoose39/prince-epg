9:45am - UEFA Champions League Football
Highlights from a thrilling, final round of fixtures in the league stage of the Champions League as teams battle to secure a place in the knockout rounds. The top eight teams go straight through to the round of 16, sides placed ninth to 24 qualify for a two-legged knockout stage, with the remaining 12 clubs eliminated. All five British clubs are in action, with two meeting at Villa Park as Celtic travel to Birmingham to take on Aston Villa for the first time in a competitive fixture.
