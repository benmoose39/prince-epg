8:00pm - Top Gear
The three presenters are challenged to drive from the heart of Switzerland to the north-western coast of England on just one tank of fuel. As an incentive, the first one to arrive gets to turn on the Blackpool Illuminations. Back at the track, James May tackles his first-ever power test in the Pagani Zonda F Roadster and, after years of waiting, The Sting gets to set a lap time in the Bugatti Veyron.
