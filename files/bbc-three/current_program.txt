8:00pm - Celebrity Race Across the World
After scooping this year's BAFTA for best factual entertainment show, the wildly popular Celebrity Race Across the World is back. Four new famous faces will be embarking on an adventure of a lifetime, a journey that will be harder than ever yet equally rewarding. They'll be leaving behind their luxurious lifestyles for an epic 12,500km race from the Amazon rainforest all the way to the Andes at ground level.
