8:35am - Deadly 60
Steve and the crew head to Mexico's breathtaking Yucatan Peninsula. He embarks on a high-octane chase with the fastest fish in the sea, the sailfish. He also dives on a pristine reef in search of a real-life sea monster. Finally, in a thrilling climax to his trip ventures alone, in complete darkness, into the `cave of hanging serpents', where he marvels at the awesome hunting skills of the Mexican Night Snake.
