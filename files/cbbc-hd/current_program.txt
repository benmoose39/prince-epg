11:00am - Horrible Histories
Alex Scott joins Rattus for a special sport-themed show, marking the 2020 Olympics. The original Olympics weren't actually much to do with sport at all, and so our show begins in Ancient Greece, where the Festival Committee is looking to pep-up its Religious Festival celebrating Zeus with the inclusion of some new events? and who better to help them figure out what those events should be than Sophie and Sebastian?! Did you know that sport has been banned at various times in History?
