11:40am - Ramsay's Kitchen Nightmares USA
Gordon travels to Queens, New York to visit PJ's Steakhouse, a restaurant he quickly discovers is all sizzle and no steak. Owners Joe and Madalyn have found themselves in serious debt after opening the high-end restaurant. The couple were overcome by grief when Joe's brother PJ passed away. Struggling to move on they decided to take over the lease of PJ's bar and turn it into a restaurant.
