10:40am - Ramsay's Kitchen Nightmares USA
Gordon visits Mojito, a Cuban cafe in Brooklyn, New York. Owners Marcelo and Kata used to be married to each other but now fight over everything. Seven years ago, the couple were happy newlyweds and the restaurant was their shared dream, but now the passion has gone and they're constantly at each other's throats, with their staff getting caught in the crossfire.
