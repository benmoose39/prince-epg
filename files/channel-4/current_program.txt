11:10am - Ramsay's Kitchen Nightmares USA
Gordon visits Anna Vincenzo's, an Italian restaurant in Boca Raton, Florida. Owner CeCe opened up the restaurant with the help of her father in 2001. Things were going well up until two years ago when the business started to slow down and CeCe is puzzled about what went wrong. Gordon tries to rescue the failing business but is shocked to discover CeCe has lost her way in the kitchen.
