11:05pm - 	War for the Planet of the Apes
Following the apes' dawn, human-simian relations are not going well. Caesar's forest-dwelling tribe are being hunted down by a ruthless, half-mad colonel obsessed with wiping their kind from the face of the earth, and it isn't long before the conflict results in tragic losses. Consumed by rage, Caesar abandons his noble, peace-seeking ways and sets out to avenge the fallen - whatever the cost.
