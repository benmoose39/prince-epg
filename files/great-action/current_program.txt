9:00pm - 	Pistol Whipped
Following an elite ex-cop kicked off the force amid accusations of theft. Troubled by heavy gambling debts and alcoholism, the man is offered salvation by a mysterious stranger who requests just a few favours in return. In exchange for freedom from debt, the former officer must dispatch some of the city's toughest criminals. However, the arrangement hits a snag when the name of a police detective appears on the hit list.
