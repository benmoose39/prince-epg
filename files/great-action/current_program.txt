2:01pm - 	City Slickers II: The Legend of Curly's Gold
Comic western sequel which sees the return of the three adventurous city boys, who are now running a radio station outside New York. The discovery of a treasure map provides the impetus for a trip to Montana and the start of another battle with nature. When a couple of lowlifes attempt to steal their map, a familiar face comes to their aid, but the stranger is not who he appears to be.
