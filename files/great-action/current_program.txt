4:01pm - 	Robbers' Roost
Jim Wall is a taciturn outlaw who lands an honest job on the spread managed by physically disabled Hank Hayes. When Bull Herrick's sister Helen is kidnapped by crooked Hank and Heesman, Jim, seeing an opportunity to redeem himself, rides to her rescue. Fortunately for our hero, Hank and Heesman are already at each other's throats.
