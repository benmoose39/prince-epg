3:00pm - 	Cowboy
A Chicago hotel clerk is dramatically transformed from tenderfoot to hardened trail hand when he goes on a cattle drive with a range boss he befriends. When the clerk loans the cowboy some money for a poker game, he is repaid with an invitation to join in a roundup. In the course of the eventful adventure, the clerk's romantic notions of cowboy life in the Wild West are permanently dispelled.
