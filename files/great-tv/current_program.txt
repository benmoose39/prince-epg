9:00pm - Randall and Hopkirk (Deceased)
Claire Faringham learns for the first time that her nephew Marty Hopkirk is dead when she arrives at his office and meets Jeff Randall and Marty's widow, Jean. Nevertheless, the purpose of her visit is not changed. She wants protection during a forthcoming trip to Monte Carlo where, she says confidently, she is going to win a fortune with a system she has perfected since her husband's death.
