9:00pm - Million Pound Pawn
In affluent Cheshire, Kathy Lyon's approached by divorced businesswoman Namita who is hoping to sell her gold wedding jewellery, while in Kensington, dealers Adam and Emily are offered a Chanel handbag in trade for one of the most exclusive bags in the world.
