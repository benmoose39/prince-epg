7:00pm - John and Lisa's Food Trip Down Under
Travel series following foodie couple John Torode MBE and Lisa Faulkner as they explore beautiful Western Australia to discover the food and wine producers putting this region on the world's culinary map - in this episode of the show, John and Lisa start their trip down south with homecooked breakfasts at an Aussie family-run cafe before discovering tasty short rib wraps at a restaurant that is ringing the culinary changes.
