7:00pm - John and Lisa's Food Trip Down Under
Travel series following foodie couple John Torode MBE and Lisa Faulkner as they explore beautiful Western Australia to discover the food and wine producers putting this region on the world's culinary map - in this episode of the show, John and Lisa explore the bush with a picnic of artisan cheese, encounter the ultimate Aussie animal, and are introduced to a unique truffle sauce that perfectly complements crayfish.
