8:10am - Abbey Clancy: Celebrity Homes
Cheshire Housewife and entrepreneur Seema Malhotra reveals how she and her family have lovingly transformed their Cheshire mansion into a stylish and comfy home by doing all the interiors themselves, and fashion designer Alice Temperley takes Abbey on a magical tour of her Alice in Wonderland-style fairytale home in Somerset with its explosion of colour and textiles, disco balls inside and out, and donkeys roaming around the dining room.
