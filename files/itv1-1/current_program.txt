Mark Pougatch presents coverage of the 2024-25 Carabao Cup semi-final second leg between Liverpool and Tottenham Hotspur - Lucas Bergvall scored the only goal of the game in the first leg to give Ange Postecoglou's side a slender advantage to take to Anfield. Expert analysis on the night comes from Robbie Fowler and Les Ferdinand, and commentary is provided by Sam Matterface and Ally McCoist.
