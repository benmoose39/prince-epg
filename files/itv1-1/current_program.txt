3:30pm - Pictionary
Mel Giedroyc hosts the game show where two teams of three players compete against each other trying to guess the correct word or phrase represented by the drawings of their fellow teammates - Mel is joined by two different celebrity team captains for each episode helping the contestants go through to the final to potentially win a holiday. In this edition of the programme, Mel is joined by Hayley Tamaddon and Dan Whiston as the players draw and their team members guess.
