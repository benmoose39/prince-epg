8:30am - Love Your Weekend with Alan Titchmarsh
Joining Alan this time are broadcasting legend Angela Rippon and musician and Spandau Ballet star Gary Kemp - Camilla Bassett-Smith opens up her February diary with some fragrant flowers perfect for planting at this time of year, herb extraordinaire Jekka McVicar brews up some homemade 'love potions', farmer Tom Davis shows us how he gets his purebred poultry competition-ready, and Jaega Wise shares her 'top of the hops' with some winter-warming beers in the Best of British drinks feature.
