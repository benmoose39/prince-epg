1:00pm - 
Ed Chamberlin presents coverage of racing from Sandown, Musselburgh and Leopardstown - Sandown hosts five races including the latest Grade One event of the UK season in the form of the Scilly Isles Novices' Chase, and there is also competitive action from Musselburgh while the best race of the day at Leopardstown is the Grade One Irish Gold Cup. Analysis comes from Mick Fitzgerald and Luke Harvey, reporting from Alice Plunkett and Matt Chapman, and commentary from Mark Johnson.
