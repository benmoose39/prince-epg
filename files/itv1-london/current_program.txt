Oli Bell presents coverage of racing from Leopardstown and Musselburgh - the feature meeting of the day is at Leopardstown where the Irish Champion Hurdle takes centre stage on the second day of the 2025 Dublin Racing Festival, and there are also four competitive races from Musselburgh. Analysis comes from Matt Chapman and Megan Nicholls, reporting from Sally Ann Grassick, and commentary from Richard Hoiles.
