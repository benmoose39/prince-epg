7:50am - Karen Carney's Leaders of the Pack
From humble beginnings of playing barefoot on the streets to proudly captaining her country, Rafaelle Souza's remarkable journey has been anything but straightforward - a tenacious and fearless defender, she has left her mark on football pitches across four different continents. Karen catches up with this exceptional athlete as she opens up about her extraordinary career and ambitions of leading Brazil to their first-ever victory in the FIFA Women's World Cup.
