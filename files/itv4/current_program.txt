12:45pm - 
Jacqui Oatley presents coverage of the 2025 Masters from the Marshall Arena in Milton Keynes, an event which features 32 of the top players from around the world in its newly-extended format - this session sees eight players compete in the second round for a place in the quarter-finals. Match analysis is by Chris Mason, Alan Warriner-Little and Mark Webster, and commentary and interviews come from Ned Boulting, Stuart Pyke, and John Rawling.
