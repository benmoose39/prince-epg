Jacqui Oatley presents coverage of the 2025 Masters from the Marshall Arena in Milton Keynes - in its newly-extended format, it features 32 of the top players from around the world including newly-crowned world champion Luke Littler. The Masters will also now count towards the PDC Order of Merit and be the first ranked event of the new campaign - the second day sees 16 more players go head-to-head for a place in the second round.
