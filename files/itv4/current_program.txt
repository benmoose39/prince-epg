Hugh Woozencroft presents coverage of 2024-25 EFL Championship football as local rivals Middlesbrough face Sunderland - Middlesbrough are aiming to secure bragging rights over their northeast neighbours as they bid for promotion and to end eight seasons in the EFL, while Sunderland are on the push for automatic promotion and looking to seal their first win at the Riverside Stadium in 13 years.
