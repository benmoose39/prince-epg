8:25am - UIM E1 World Championship
Highlights as the second season of the world's only all-electric race boat championship kicks off in Jeddah in Saudi Arabia - this season sees nine teams competing, each with male and female pilots, and are owned by some of the biggest names on the planet. They include Will Smith, Marc Anthony and Steve Aoki from the world of entertainment, Rafael Nadal, Sergio Perez, Tom Brady, Virat Kohli and Didier Drogba from the world of sport, and Marcelo Claure from the world of entrepreneurship.
