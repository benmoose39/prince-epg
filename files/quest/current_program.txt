11:00am - The Repair Shop
Luthier Julyan Wallis gets to grips with a semi acoustic guitar that has spanned three generations. Pete Rout and his son, Tom, put their faith in Julyan to save the beloved instrument that has been silent for over 40 years. First played by Pete's father, it was passed it on to Pete when he was a young man, who then learnt to play it himself.
