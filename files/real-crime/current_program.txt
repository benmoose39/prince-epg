7:34pm - 	Gangster No. 1: The Freddie Foreman Story
Attached to almost every unsolved gangland murder in London over the last 50 years, Freddie Foreman is the `Godfather' of Britain's criminal underworld. Foreman reveals the audacious details of the heists, double crossings, shootouts, murders and betrayals he was involved in, and ultimately, what has made him `Gangster Number.1'.
