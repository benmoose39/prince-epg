7:55am - 	Phantom Ship
The mystery of the 'Mary Celeste' will forever remain an enigma to the world. In PHANTOM SHIP, Denison Clift attempts to shed light on the unknown - and comes up with a masterpiece of early cinema. The drama begins at New York Harbor, where the Mary Celeste is ready to sail. The voyage is beset with murder, mutiny and misfortune as one inexplicable event follows another in quick succession.
