9:55pm - Shameless
In an attempt to build confidence, Ian joins the local boxing club and quickly strikes up a friendship with the owner, Jock. However, when the club faces closure due to Jock's debt with Paddy, Ian enlists Maxine, Carl and Chesney to help raise funds. It soon becomes clear that Ian has stumbled into a hornet's nest of unfinished business that exposes the true nature of Paddy and Jock's relationship.
