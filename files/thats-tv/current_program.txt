9:55pm - Shameless
Liam befriends World War Two veteran, Reggie, to help him with a school project; troubled and lonely without his sister, and fed up with Libby's well-intentioned interference at home, Liam finds solace in his friendship with the old man. However, when Libby spies on Liam and his secret friend, she jumps to the all the wrong conclusions - leading to a tragic confrontation between the Gallaghers and Reggie.
