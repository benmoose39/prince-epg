9:00pm - Benidorm
Probate attorney Buck A Roo tells the Garveys that a multi-million inheritance is Madge's for the taking, but they reject him as a fraud. The eavesdropping Solana staff are gobsmacked, and Joyce sends Lesley off to do some research on Buck. Glynn Flint reveals more about life with wife Rhiannon to a sympathetic though bored Jacqueline, while Clive Dyke makes the most of being single and tries his luck with a holidaymaker.
