5:00pm - Edwardian Farm
It is February and the team approach the half way point in their year on the Edwardian Farm. To mark the occasion this episode explores one single day in a typical Edwardian farmer's life. Incorporating a remarkable cache of letters written in the 1900s in a cottage at Morwhellham Quay, `A Day in the Life' reveals the hidden stories of how ordinary rural Edwardians got by.
